Choose mode

MoviePicker allows you to choose between two modes of operation, so you can find the ideal movie to watch alone or with friends!

Discovery Mode

Discovery mode allows you to browse through the various movie offerings. You will have to decide whether you like or dislike the proposed movie or whether you have finally found what you were looking for.

You won't have to do anything else, our algorithm will do the rest: find the movies most akin to the movies you've liked.

WatchNow Mode New

The watchNow mode allows you to choose from several movie suggestions recommended by our algorithm. Here's where the fun comes in: you'll have to like 16 movies that will be proposed to you, after which the tournament phase will be activated.

The rules are simple: the movies in the tournament are presented in pairs, you decide which one to keep and continue like that until the end. 16 movies, but only one winner!
